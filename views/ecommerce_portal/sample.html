Program Highlights

No More Paper!

Every month, you'll receive an email when your DTE Energy statement is available online at your DTE Energy account. This free service is not only good our palnet, it saves you the hassle of recycling.

Integrated With Other Programs

Monthly Remainders

When your DTE Energy bill is ready, you will receive an email when your eBill paperless billing statement is ready for viewing, usually 17-20 days before the due date.

eBill Paperless Billing FAQ

How do I cancel eBill Paperless billing?

You may unenroll from eBill paperless billing at any time. Once your cancellation request has been processed, you will begin receiving a paper bill again.

Enrollment requirements

Upon activation, you will no longer receive a paper bill unless your DTE Energy account is in shut-off status. Instead, you will receive a monthly email reminder when your bill is available online. You may unenroll from eBill paperless billing at any time.
